    Recipe Highlight — Delimundo Corned Beef

    Ingredients:
    1 Onion 1 tbsp Minced Garlic 1 Can of Delimundo Ranch Style Corned Beef
    1 tbsp Salt 1/2 cup Milk Chives or Parsley

    Procedure:

    It's straightforward but there are a few specific tips and steps to make it perfect:

    1. Cut potatoes into equal pieces.
    2. Boil in salted water until very soft and tender.
    3. Drain, return to pot, and mash with butter and milk or cream.
    4. Mash until creamy and fluffy.
    5. Transfer to serving bowl and make gentle swirls on top.
    6. Drizzle with butter for extra richness and presentation!
